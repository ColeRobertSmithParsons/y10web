Leafs Talk

With Cole Parsons


Auston Matthews

Matthews is hands down one of the brightest talents in the NHL, recently he has struggled, only scoring 1 goal in his last 9 games, this is a rare occurance for the Leafs Allstar, he will hope to gain his stride come after the all star break, and return to the dominant centre that we all know and love.



John Tavares

Tavares has been everything that leafs have hoped for since signing him in the offseason, the dominant centre is on pace for 100+ points, and most impressively 60+ goals, truly living up to his higher than high standards set by the leafs faithful.
